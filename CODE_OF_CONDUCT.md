# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We are committed to a welcoming, safe, and constructive project. Participate
with empathy, assume good intent while evaluating evidence, respect differing
experience levels, and give actionable technical feedback. Examples of
unacceptable behavior include harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized
attention, publishing another person's private information, deliberate
disruption, and pressuring anyone to disclose credentials or private code.

## Scope

This standard applies in the repository, issues, pull requests, discussions, and
other spaces where someone represents Duet. Maintainers may remove content,
close interactions, restrict participation, or report abuse to GitHub when
behavior threatens people or the project.

## Reporting

Use GitHub's block and abuse-reporting tools for urgent safety concerns. For
project moderation, contact the maintainer through the private contact methods
available on the [maintainer profile](https://github.com/chriswu727). Do not use
a public issue to disclose personal information. Reports will be considered in
good faith and handled as privately as the available channel permits.

This policy is informed by the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
